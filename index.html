Who am I

Hello there, myself Sanchari Sen, a final year undergraduate student pursuing B.Sc in Physics (Honours) at St. Xavier's College (Autonomous), Kolkata.

College life opened up a whole lot of new doors of exploration for me. My interests are mostly in certain aspects of Astrophysics- the beautiful blend of Particle Physics and Cosmology, Nuclear Physics in Astrophysical domains and Computational methods. Outside academia, you might find me with my camera, reading fiction, writing blogs or travelling.

Academia

I have grown up in Kolkata, India and completed high school in 2022 from St. Stephen's School, Dum Dum. Soon I will be starting my final year of undergraduate at St. Xavier's College (Autonomous), Kolkata.

Rsearch Interests:

1. Cosmology-I am working on a project at the Xaverian Astronomical Society titled "Constraining Cosmological Parameters using Bayesian Inference". Primarily based on Newtonian Cosmology, here I am trying to apply Bayesian Statistical methods on Type 1 Supernovae data and estimate certain cosmological parameters of the Î›CDM model.
I also undertook a study project where I worked on developing a pipeline for the removal of Foreground from 21 cm Cosmological Signals using GPR.

2. Nuclear Physics: I developed a code in Python which plots the kinematics of multiple two body nuclear reactions, considering all the specifications such as input energy, target thickness and thus can compare and contrast different reactions.
Currently, I am developing the Monte Carlo Simulation to study the breakup of light nuclei relevant to nuclear astrophysics.

Apart from these projects, I am actively engaged in STEM activities. For the past two years I have been participating as a student speaker in a summer school on Astronomy for high school students. I am also a content editor at two peer reviewed journals for young STEM enthusiasts.

What else

"All work and no play makes Jack a dull boy" is something I strongly believe in. Outside academics I spend my time reading (strictly fiction! Because who doesn't love a good escape!). I am also an ametuer photographer, experimenting with my camera in different settings (check it out here!)-quite convenient since I love to travel a lot.
